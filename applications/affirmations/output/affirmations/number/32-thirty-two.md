# Thirty Two Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was aligned with divine order
2. I followed the unfolding divine plan
3. I completed the necessary life transition

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I align with divine order now
2. I accept this unfolding of my life
3. I follow the path of divine design

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will follow divine order in my unfolding life
2. I will accept this transition toward my true path
3. I will achieve wholeness through divine rhythm

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been unfolding divine order through my journey
2. I have been integrating brokenness into my wholeness
3. I have been aligning my steps with cosmic design

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have navigated trials toward divine completion
2. I will have embodied divine order in my life
3. I will have realized the unfolding of God's perfect plan

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We follow divine order in our unfolding journey
2. We embrace the pattern of life together now
3. We complete our path through divine design

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They follow the divine order in their journey
2. She embraces transition with purposeful grace
3. The path unfolds through balanced experience

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can follow divine order in my life
2. I am able to complete my life's journey
3. I can align with the unfolding divine plan

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I embrace divine order in my unfolding life
2. If only I could follow the divine path now
3. Would that I might align with cosmic rhythm

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Embrace divine order and unfold your true path
2. Align your soul with the grand divine rhythm now
3. Trust the unfolding sequence toward your wholeness

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not flowing with divine order now
2. Have I not accepted this unfolding journey
3. Can I not trust this purposeful path

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To align with divine order always
2. To move through trials purposefully
3. To achieve wholeness through rhythm

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Embracing transitions unfolds divine order within me
2. Navigating trials reveals purposeful unfolding of life
3. Aligning with rhythm completes the divine design

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am guided by divine order in my unfolding
2. I am held by the rhythm of life's progression
3. I am shaped by trials toward my true fulfillment

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always follow divine order in my journey
2. I regularly embrace life's unfolding pattern
3. I consistently trust the path of my creation

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not broken but follow divine order now
2. I do not resist the unfolding of my path
3. I am not lost but trust the divine sequence
