# Sixteen Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I saw the divine pattern unfolding before me
2. I recognized the interconnectedness of all things
3. I accepted the balance of the divine structure

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I see the divine pattern unfolding now
2. I recognize interconnectedness in all things
3. I align with the divine order presently

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will perceive the divine structure of all things
2. I will recognize interconnectedness in my existence
3. I will align with the unfolding divine pattern

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been seeing divine patterns unfolding within me
2. I have been recognizing interconnectedness in all things
3. I have been aligning with the unfolding divine structure

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have perceived the divine structure fully
2. I will have integrated all interconnected patterns now
3. I will have realized my place in the whole

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We see the divine pattern unfolding together
2. We recognize interconnectedness in all things now
3. We align with the unfolding divine order always

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. The structure reveals divine interconnectedness in all things
2. Balance unfolds through recognizing layered spiritual patterns
3. Order manifests within the unfolding divine narrative

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can see the divine pattern unfolding now
2. I could recognize interconnected spiritual realities easily
3. I am able to align with the cosmic balance always

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I see the divine pattern unfolding now
2. If only I could grasp this deep connection
3. Would that I might align with cosmic balance

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. See the pattern within all divine structures
2. Recognize the interconnectedness of all realities
3. Align with the unfolding divine order now

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not seeing the divine pattern now
2. Have I not recognized interconnectedness fully
3. Can I not align with this unfolding order

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To perceive the divine structure without illusion
2. To recognize interconnectedness in all things
3. To unfold the pattern of divine progression

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Seeing patterns reveals divine interconnectedness
2. Understanding symbols unfolds the divine order
3. Embracing balance reflects unfolding cosmic reality

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am held by divine structure by pattern
2. I am seen by unfolding reality by order
3. I am grounded by interconnectedness by balance

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always see the divine pattern unfolding now
2. I regularly recognize interconnected spiritual reality
3. I consistently align with the unfolding divine order

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not limited by surface appearances
2. I do not seek only the visible outcome
3. I am not separate from the divine pattern
