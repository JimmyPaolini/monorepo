# Chesed (Mercy) Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was boundless mercy in my giving
2. I gave infinite compassion to all
3. I nurtured growth through open acceptance

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I give boundless mercy to all beings
2. I flow with cosmic, expansive giving now
3. I possess infinite capacity for growth

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will give boundless mercy to all beings
2. I will receive infinite flow of cosmic growth
3. I will expand my heart with nurturing acceptance

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been giving boundless mercy to all beings
2. I have been receiving infinite compassion from others
3. I have been expanding my capacity for generous giving

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have given boundless mercy to all beings
2. I will have become a source of infinite compassion
3. I will have nurtured growth through generous giving

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We give boundless mercy to each other always
2. We share infinite compassion in our connection
3. We allow mutual growth through open giving

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. The branches flow with boundless cosmic giving
2. Mercy expands through mutual interconnectedness
3. Compassion nurtures all forms of growth

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can give boundless mercy to everyone now
2. I am able to share infinite compassion freely always
3. I can receive and give with open hearts

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I give boundless mercy to all beings
2. Would that I might flow with infinite compassion
3. If only I could receive abundant grace now

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Give boundless mercy to all beings now
2. Receive infinite compassion in every moment
3. Expand your giving with open acceptance

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not giving boundless mercy freely now
2. Have I not accepted all cosmic flow without fear
3. Can I not expand my giving without measure

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To give boundless mercy always
2. To receive infinite compassion freely
3. To expand growth without limitation

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Giving compassion expands boundless opportunities
2. Receiving mercy fosters deep inner connection
3. Nurturing growth unfolds infinite potential

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am held by boundless mercy and grace
2. I am nurtured by infinite giving and flow
3. I am surrounded by compassionate acceptance

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always give boundless mercy to all beings
2. I regularly share my abundance with others
3. I consistently accept and offer infinite compassion

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not limited by giving without measure
2. I do not hold back my boundless compassion now
3. I am not lacking in infinite giving always
