# Atziluth (Emanation) Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was pure divine essence in that moment
2. I reflected the source of all being then
3. I knew absolute unity in that state

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I possess pure divine essence now
2. I reflect the undifferentiated oneness always
3. I experience the source of all being

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will recognize divine unity within all existence
2. I will embody pure light and undifferentiated oneness
3. I will flow from the source of all being

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been flowing from pure divine essence always
2. I have been reflecting the unified divine light always
3. I have been embodying the undifferentiated oneness always

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have known the divine source completely
2. I will have reflected pure unity in all being
3. I will have become the flowing divine essence

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We are pure divine unity in this moment
2. We reflect the source of all existence now
3. We embody the unmanifest divine flow

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. The divine essence flows from pure unity
2. Light manifests as undifferentiated being
3. Existence reflects the source of oneness

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can access pure divine unity now
2. I am able to reflect the source of all being
3. I can embody the unmanifest divine light

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I know this pure divine unity now
2. If only I could feel the source's essence
3. Would that I might rest in perfect oneness

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Know your oneness now
2. Receive the pure light always
3. Embrace the divine flow within

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not pure divine light now
2. Have I not experienced absolute oneness always
3. Can I not flow from the source freely

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To recognize unity without separation
2. To embody pure divine essence always
3. To flow from the source without knowing

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Embodying unity flows pure divine essence now
2. Receiving source reveals inherent oneness always
3. Vibrating as pure light manifests all being

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am known by divine essence
2. I am held by pure light
3. I am grounded by unity

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always reflect divine unity in my being
2. I regularly experience pure divine essence now
3. I consistently embody the source of all reality

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not separate from the divine source
2. I do not hold limited form or shape
3. I am not defined by external experience
