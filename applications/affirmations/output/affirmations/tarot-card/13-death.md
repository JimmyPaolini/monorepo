# Death Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was willing to let go of the old self
2. I chose to surrender my identity
3. I found the courage to die

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am ready to let go of the old self
2. I choose to embrace the new beginning
3. I surrender completely to the new life

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will surrender completely before the transformation begins
2. I will embrace my potential without the old self
3. I will find peace through the surrender of the old identity

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been shedding the flesh of my old persona to reveal the skeleton of my true self
2. I have been surrendering my ego to allow the new life to emerge from the deep
3. I have been letting go of the past relationship to clear the ground for a fundamentally new love

2. I will have created a new identity that replaces the old persona
3. I will have surrendered the old self to allow the new one to rise
