# The Ten of Cups Affirmations

1. I was grateful for the warmth of my family
2. I chose to embrace the peace of my inner world
3. I found my strength in the unity of my community

1. I am worthy of love
2. I trust myself
3. I am confident

3. I will embrace my potential

1. I have been cherishing the bonds of my family unit

1. I can build a home where every family member feels loved and supported
