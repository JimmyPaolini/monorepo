# The Five of Cups Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was gentle enough to accept the loss of the cup
2. I chose to find strength in the bridge of the river
3. I found my courage in the spilled cups of the past

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am ready to let go of the past
2. I trust that the future is open to me
3. I am whole enough to welcome new beginnings

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will find peace in the quiet of the cup
2. I will embrace the loss of the cup
3. I will find healing in the bridge of the cup

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been learning to let go of the fear of losing my friends
2. I have been choosing kindness over the pain of missing loved ones
3. I have been growing every day in the quiet of the empty cup

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have overcome the loss of the cup that fell
2. I will have become the person who learned to accept the cup that fell
3. I will have created a life that flows through the depths of the cup that fell

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We are healing together
2. We are connected
3. We are resilient

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They are ready to move forward despite the heavy weight of their past
2. She finds peace in knowing that her struggles are part of a healing journey
3. They trust that the capacity to heal is a natural outcome of the process itself

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can heal my heart by letting go of the past
2. I am able to find peace in the present moment
3. I could become the vessel of healing

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I find peace within the black cocoon that waits for me
2. If only I could see the spilled cups as real gifts rather than lost things
3. Would that I might know that healing is a flowing river, not a static shore

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Be gentle with your heart today
2. Trust that your healing is flowing
3. Embrace the flow of your journey

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not capable of letting go of the past?
2. Have I not already found peace within the cup?
3. Can I not trust that healing flows forward?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To let go of the fear of losing again
2. To embrace the reality of what has been lost
3. To choose connection over judgment

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Being gentle with loss is my strength
2. Accepting disappointment is my power
3. Finding healing in the cup is my way

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am supported by the wisdom of the past
2. I am guided by the flow of the present
3. I am loved by the grace of the future

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always choose kindness when I feel lost
2. I regularly practice gratitude even when things are disappointing
3. I consistently show up for myself during the darkest times

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not defined by the loss of my loved ones
2. I am not afraid of the disappointment of my past choices
3. I am not limited by the fear of the future's outcome
