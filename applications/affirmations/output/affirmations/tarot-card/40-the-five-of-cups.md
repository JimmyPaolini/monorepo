# The Five of Cups Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I felt deep sorrow for what I lost
2. I acknowledged the pain of my past regrets
3. I saw the blessings remaining in my life

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I accept my present feelings of sorrow now
2. I focus my energy on what remains
3. I appreciate the blessings I currently hold

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will accept my present blessings now
2. I will focus on what remains in my life
3. I will move past past sorrow and regret

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been feeling the sorrow of past losses
2. I have been accepting what remains in my life
3. I have been redirecting my focus to present blessings

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have accepted my past losses now
2. I will have focused on present blessings always
3. I will have found peace within this moment

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We accept our past losses together now
2. We focus on present blessings with grace
3. We value what remains in this moment

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They accept their past losses with gentle sorrow
2. She focuses her energy on present blessings now
3. They recognize the beauty in what remains

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can accept present blessings now
2. I could focus on what remains
3. I am able to release past sorrow

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I accept my present blessings now
2. If only I could release past sorrow
3. Would that I might focus on what remains

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Accept your sorrow now!
2. Focus on what remains today!
3. Value the blessings you have

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not dwelling on past losses now?
2. Have I not accepted what remains today?
3. Can I not focus on present blessings only?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To accept sorrow without dwelling on loss
2. To focus on remaining blessings now
3. To find peace within present moments

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Grieving losses allows accepting present blessings
2. Focusing on remaining cups brings inner peace
3. Processing sorrow redirects energy to joy

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am sorrow by loss held
2. I am regret by pain carried
3. I am acceptance by blessings surrounded

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always acknowledge my past sorrow and loss
2. I regularly focus on present blessings now
3. I consistently value what remains in my life

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I do not focus on what is lost now
2. I am not defined by past disappointments
3. I do not dwell on sorrow anymore
