# The Nine of Pentacles Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was self-sufficient in my beautiful living
2. I cultivated my success through my labor
3. I enjoyed the fruits of my hard work

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I enjoy my cultivated and beautiful life
2. I possess the peace of my self-sufficiency
3. I appreciate the fruits of my hard work

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will enjoy my cultivated beautiful life
2. I will appreciate my earned personal success
3. I will live in serene self-sufficiency now

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been cultivating my beautiful personal space
2. I have been enjoying the fruits of my hard labor
3. I have been appreciating my self-sufficient comfort

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have cultivated beautiful spaces for myself
2. I will have achieved serene self-sufficiency now
3. I will have earned this luxurious, peaceful existence

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We enjoy our cultivated spaces together
2. We appreciate our earned personal success together
3. We find deep contentment in our independence together

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. She cultivates beauty in her own refined spaces
2. They enjoy the fruits of their hard-earned labor
3. He possesses serene contentment from self-reliance

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can enjoy my cultivated beautiful life now
2. I am able to appreciate my earned self-sufficiency
3. I can possess deep inner contentment freely

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I enjoy my cultivated beautiful life now
2. If only I could feel this deep contentment always
3. Would that I might appreciate my earned success

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Enjoy your cultivated beauty yourself
2. Appreciate your earned independence now
3. Savor your peaceful self-sufficiency always

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not content with my cultivated beauty?
2. Have I not earned this peaceful self-sufficiency?
3. Can I not enjoy my well-deserved abundance?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To enjoy your cultivated beauty calmly
2. To appreciate your earned independence fully
3. To live in serene self-sufficiency always

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Cultivating beauty brings deep personal satisfaction
2. Enjoying abundance fosters serene self-reliance
3. Appreciating labor reveals inner contentment

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am cultivated by my own labor
2. I am surrounded by serene comfort
3. I am held by my earned independence

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always enjoy my cultivated beautiful surroundings
2. I regularly appreciate my earned personal success
3. I consistently find deep contentment in my independence

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not lacking in my cultivated beauty
2. I do not need external validation for my wealth
3. I am not dependent on others for my comfort
