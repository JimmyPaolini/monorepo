# The Nine of Pentacles Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was confident enough to build a stable foundation for my own success
2. I chose to embrace the joy of creating a secure and enduring home
3. I found my strength in maintaining a life of abundance and self-sufficiency

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am confident in my own ability to create a stable foundation for my life
2. I am worthy of the joy and peace that comes from maintaining my own status and growth
3. I trust myself to enjoy the fruits of my hard work and maintain my dignity

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will find peace
2. I will succeed
3. I will achieve my potential

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been cultivating a stable foundation that allows me to enjoy the fruits of my labor without external dependency
2. I have been growing every day with the confidence that all feelings are reciprocated and that the relationship thrives on the appreciation of the partner's presence
3. I have been building my resilience by creating a solid material foundation that ensures the pursuit of success is a joyful and sustainable endeavor

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have achieved the mastery of my craft
2. I will have become the expert who guides others
3. I will have created a life of abundance and joy

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We are confident in our own strength and wisdom
2. We build a stable foundation for our shared future
3. We create abundance and joy together

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. He is confident in his own ability to create a stable foundation for his life
2. She trusts that her hard work will yield a secure and joyful future for her
3. They are assured that their shared success will bring them a rich and harmonious relationship

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am able to build a stable foundation for my own success
2. I can enjoy the fruits of my own hard work without external dependency
3. I am confident in my ability to create a life of abundance and self-sufficiency

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I find my own worth in this moment
2. If only I could feel at peace with my own path
3. Would that I might know my own value

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Trust yourself! You are worthy of your own success
2. Embrace your strength! You have the power to create abundance
3. Be bold! Your hard work will pay off in a beautiful way

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not capable of creating a stable foundation for my own success?
2. Have I not already proven my resilience in maintaining my dignity?
3. Can I not rise to this challenge without external dependency?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To build a foundation of stability where I can enjoy the fruits of my own labor
2. To maintain a life of self-sufficiency where I can pursue my goals without external dependency
3. To cultivate a relationship built on mutual respect and deep emotional intimacy

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Being a self-sufficient individual is my strength
2. Creating a stable foundation is my strength
3. Being a partner in a shared future is my strength

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am supported by the wisdom of the stars
2. I am guided by the clarity of the path
3. I am loved by the harmony of the world

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always maintain a stable foundation that allows me to enjoy the fruits of my hard work
2. I consistently work diligently to build a secure and prosperous life for myself
3. I regularly pursue self-sufficiency to ensure I can live a joyful and independent life

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not defined by my past
2. I am not afraid of change
3. I do not limit my own potential
