# The Hermit Affirmations

1. I sought inner light through quiet reflection
2. I found wisdom in my solitary journey
3. I explored my soul's truth alone

1. I seek inner wisdom through quiet reflection
2. I illuminate my path by deep introspection
3. I discover my truth within my solitude
