# The Seven of Wands Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was brave enough to stand my ground
2. I chose my own path without fear
3. I found my strength in my own will

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am ready to act without listening to others
2. I trust my own judgment to guide my path
3. I am strong enough to stand alone

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will choose to act without listening to others
2. I will stand back in line to build my own life
3. I will find peace by removing the tyrant from my mind

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been learning to trust my own voice without listening to the noise of others
2. I have been growing every day by choosing kindness over the fear of losing control
3. I have been building resilience by removing the tyrant from my own mind

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have overcome the tyranny of external dictates
2. I will have become the warrior I am meant to be
3. I will have created a life I love without the tyrant

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We stand firm together against the tyranny of external pressure
2. We choose our own path without fear of losing control
3. We build our own life by removing the tyrant from our minds

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They are ready to act without hesitation
2. They choose to be their own voice
3. They stand firm in their own path

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can choose to stand my ground and protect my own truth
2. I am able to act without being controlled by others
3. I could become the warrior who fights for my own path

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I find clarity in this moment?
2. If only I could feel at peace?
3. Would that I might know my own worth?

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Be bold to stand on your own two feet!
2. Trust your own judgment without fear!
3. Embrace your strength and courage!

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not capable of standing up to the tyrant of my own mind?
2. Have I not already proven my resilience when I am not afraid of the external world?
3. Can I not rise to this challenge without the tyrant of authority in my heart?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To stand firm in my own truth without fear of external pressure
2. To choose my own path without the tyranny of others
3. To act with courage and independence, free from the need to listen

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Being independent is my strength
2. Choosing courage is my strength
3. Standing up is my strength

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am guided by the truth of my own heart
2. I am supported by the quiet power within me
3. I am free to choose the path that belongs to me

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always choose kindness and stand by my own will
2. I regularly practice self-acceptance and trust my own voice
3. I consistently show up for myself and honor my true self

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not defined by my past
2. I am not afraid of change
3. I do not limit my own potential
