# The Four of Wands Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was honored by the celebration of our shared milestones
2. I chose to celebrate the progress of our team together
3. I found my strength in the joy of our collective success

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am honored by the celebration of my own milestones
2. I trust that my journey is shared and honored by the community
3. I am worthy of the joy of celebrating my own progress

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will celebrate my own milestone with joy
2. I will find peace through the shared celebration of our journey
3. I will embrace my potential as a part of a harmonious whole

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been celebrating my own growth and contributing to the team's success
2. I have been honoring the milestones shared with my partner and strengthening our bond
3. I have been embracing the joy of our shared journey and ensuring our progress is valued

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have become the person I am meant to be
2. I will have created a life I love
3. I will have overcome this challenge

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We celebrate our shared milestones together
2. We honor our collective progress
3. We stand together to celebrate our success

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They celebrate the shared victories of their team
2. They honor the collective success of their group
3. They recognize the joy of their own contributions

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am able to celebrate my own milestones with joy
2. I could share the celebration of our shared journey with you
3. I am ready to honor the progress of our team together

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I find joy in celebrating our shared milestones together
2. If only I could honor the celebration of our collective success
3. Would that I might find peace in knowing our journey is worth enjoying

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Celebrate your own journey with joy!
2. Share your success with the community!
3. Embrace your strength together!

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not capable of celebrating my own milestones without fear?
2. Have I not already proven my resilience in a shared journey?
3. Can I not rise to this challenge with the support of our community?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To celebrate our shared milestones with joy
2. To honor our collective progress together
3. To embrace the harmony of our shared journey

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Being celebrated by the team is my strength

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am honored by the celebration of our shared milestones
2. I am guided by the joy of our collective achievements

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative
