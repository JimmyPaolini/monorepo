# The Nine of Cups Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was grateful for the abundance of what I had
2. I chose peace in the quiet of my solitude
3. I found my strength in the harmony of my life

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am grateful for the abundance I hold in my hands
2. I trust that my desires are being fulfilled by the universe
3. I am ready to embrace the joy of what is now in my possession

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will find peace in the quiet of my solitude
2. I will succeed in achieving my deepest desires
3. I will embrace my potential with joy and clarity

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been cultivating gratitude for every present moment
2. I have been embracing the joy of my current abundance
3. I have been seeking the peace of inner harmony

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have achieved a life of joy and clarity
2. I will have become the person I am meant to be
3. I will have created a world where my wishes are fulfilled

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We are grateful for the abundance in our current possession
2. We are aligned with the harmony of our desires and wishes
3. We are ready to create positive change together

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They are grateful for the abundance they hold
2. They are seeking harmony in their current path
3. They are aligned with the center of the universe

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am able to find deep satisfaction in the abundance of what I already possess
2. I can choose to embrace the joy and clarity that fills my life with energy
3. I could become a seeker of fulfillment who finds peace in the harmony of my desires

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I find my own worth in this moment
2. If only I could feel at peace with this abundance
3. Would that I might know the center of the universe

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Be joyful in your abundance!
2. Trust your inner peace!
3. Embrace your full success!

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not capable of finding true satisfaction in what I already possess?
2. Have I not already proven my resilience in reconciling past conflicts and seeking inner peace?
3. Can I not rise to this challenge by aligning my desires with the universe's alignment?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To be joyful in the abundance of what I have
2. To be free from the fear of what I desire
3. To be complete in the fulfillment of what I seek

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Being grateful for the abundance in your hands is my strength
2. Choosing joy and clarity in every moment is my strength
3. Embracing the completion of your journey is my strength

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am guided by the wisdom of the past
2. I am supported by the abundance of the present
3. I am loved by the harmony of the future

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always choose gratitude for what is currently in my possession
2. I regularly practice the joy of receiving what I have
3. I consistently show up for my own abundance and success

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not defined by my past
2. I am not afraid of change
3. I do not limit my own potential
