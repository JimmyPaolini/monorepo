# The Three of Cups Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was happy to share my joy with you
2. I chose to celebrate our friendship today
3. I found my strength in our shared laughter

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am deeply grateful for the joy shared in our gatherings
2. I trust that our collective strength brings us closer together
3. I celebrate the unity of our diverse perspectives as a source of true happiness

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will find joy in sharing my happiness with my friends
2. I will celebrate my potential with my community
3. I will find prosperity through the strength of our shared bonds

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been nurturing friendships that bring me joy
2. I have been celebrating shared moments with my loved ones
3. I have been growing in community service and connection

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have created a world where friendship is the only true currency
2. I will have become the person who brings joy to every single cup in the room
3. I will have achieved a balance of love and connection that brings lasting prosperity

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We celebrate our shared joy together
2. We nurture our friendships to build prosperity
3. We unite our hearts for mutual support

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They gather diverse perspectives to create a cohesive whole
2. They celebrate the joy of shared experiences together
3. They nurture friendships alongside romantic partnerships

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am able to share joy with my friends
2. I could nurture friendships that bring us together
3. I am capable of celebrating the unity of our community

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I find joy in our shared cup of happiness
2. If only I could celebrate our friendship with you
3. Would that I might share this moment of joy with you

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Be joyful with your friends! 🎉
2. Share your happiness with everyone! 🌟
3. Celebration is a gift you receive! 🎊

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not capable of sharing this joy with you?
2. Have I not already proven my resilience through this gathering?
3. Can I not rise to this challenge with your support?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To celebrate our shared joy and unite our hearts
2. To embrace our friendship and grow together
3. To nurture our community and find our true strength

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Being connected with friends is my strength
2. Celebrating shared joy together is my strength
3. Finding happiness through community is my strength

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am supported by the gathering of diverse perspectives
2. I am loved and cherished by those who share my heart
3. I am guided by the warmth of mutual celebration

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always celebrate my friends with joy and laughter
2. I consistently share my happiness with my community
3. I regularly nurture friendships to build lasting prosperity

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not defined by my past
2. I am not afraid of change
3. I do not limit my own potential
