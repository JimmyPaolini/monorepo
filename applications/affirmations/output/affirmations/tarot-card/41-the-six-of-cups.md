# The Six of Cups Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I was happy to see the sun rise again
2. I chose peace to find my way home
3. I found my strength in the warmth of the cups

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am safe enough to explore my deepest feelings
2. I am worthy of love and being loved
3. I am ready to return to a state of peace and balance

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will find peace in the warmth of my heart
2. I will embrace my potential with courage
3. I will find harmony in the beauty of my past

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been learning to feel the warmth of love
2. I have been choosing kindness in every moment
3. I have been growing every day with the hope of healing

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have found a place where all my feelings are safe to express
2. I will have become the person who knows every emotion I feel
3. I will have created a world where love and joy are the only things that matter

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We are all connected by the same warm heart
2. We embrace every feeling with open and loving hands
3. We find peace in the harmony of all our emotions

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They are safe enough to explore their deepest feelings without judgment
2. They embrace the interconnectedness of all their emotions
3. They find a renewed capacity to love and be loved

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I am able to feel the warmth of my own heart
2. I can embrace the joy of my past experiences
3. I could become the person who feels safe and loved

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I find peace in this warm embrace of all my feelings
2. If only I could feel safe enough to explore my deepest history
3. Would that I might know my own worth within the compass of love

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Embrace your courage to feel your deepest fears
2. Trust your heart to heal the wounds of past sadness
3. Be gentle and kind to the people who hurt you

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not ready to embrace the warmth of my past emotions?
2. Have I not found a place within myself to feel the joy of my childhood?
3. Can I not trust that the healing of my heart begins with me?

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To embrace the warmth of joy and love with open hands
2. To find peace within the shadow of fear and shame
3. To walk through the past with clarity and compassion

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Being warm and inviting is my strength
2. Embracing the past is my strength
3. Being connected is my strength

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am guided by the compass of my own heart
2. I am supported by the warmth of my deepest feelings
3. I am loved by the harmony of my past experiences

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always return to the warmth of my childhood memories
2. I consistently feel safe enough to explore every feeling I have
3. I regularly embrace the healing power of all six emotions

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I am not defined by my past
2. I am not afraid of change
3. I do not limit my own potential
