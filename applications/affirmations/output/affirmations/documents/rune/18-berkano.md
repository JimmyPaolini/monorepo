Berkano which signifies Growth is a fundamental concept that speaks to the dynamic process of change, development, and advancement over time. At its core, it is not a sudden event but a continuous, incremental process where something moves from a less developed state toward a more complex, advanced, or mature state. This concept embodies the evolution of anything that exists, whether it is an individual, a society, a concept, or a physical object. It represents the realization of inherent potential, mirroring the journey from a seed to a fully realized plant or an idea moving toward becoming a functional system. Within the context of Runes, Berkano serves as the engine of progress, underpinning all dynamic systems by representing the continuous act of becoming. It is the dynamic journey from potential to reality, requiring effort, adaptation, and the successful navigation of change to achieve a higher, more complex state.

The implications of this growth principle are vast and manifest across numerous domains of human experience. In the realm of personal life, personal growth involves the psychological, emotional, and intellectual maturation of an individual. This is the ongoing process of learning new things, gaining valuable experience, developing necessary skills, and achieving a deeper sense of self-awareness. This internal evolution is crucial for navigating the complexities of existence. When applied to relationships, growth involves the deepening of bonds, the evolution of understanding between individuals, and the maturation of connections. In the context of work and business, growth refers to the increase in output, the expansion of revenue, the acquisition of market share, and the scaling of operational capacity, all driven by strategic investment and adaptation. Furthermore, biological growth is the physical increase in size, mass, or complexity observed in living organisms. Societal growth encompasses the advancement of a community, the development of necessary infrastructure, the widespread dissemination of knowledge, and the overall improvement of living standards for all members. Even in the realm of fortune, growth implies the unfolding of opportunities and the successful navigation of circumstances to achieve a more favorable state.

The symbolism associated with Berkano (Growth) is rich with imagery and archetypes that illuminate its meaning. The imagery of a seed pushing through the earth to become a plant reveals the potential held within a starting point and the necessary effort required for unfolding. The imagery of a winding path or a continuous upward trajectory speaks to the process-oriented nature of growth, emphasizing that progress is not a straight line but a journey filled with turns and adaptations. Archetypally, growth represents the unfolding of potential, the journey of becoming, and the continuous unfolding of consciousness. These symbols collectively reveal that growth is intrinsically linked to the active engagement with the world, the acceptance of change as a natural force, and the persistent effort required to transform latent potential into tangible reality. It is the constant, unfolding becoming that defines the essence of Berkano.
