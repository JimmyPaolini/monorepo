The Bouquet is a potent symbol that serves as a visual metaphor for harmony, success, and the natural blooming of life, serving as a powerful indicator of a prosperous and peaceful life path. In Lenormand, it is frequently associated with the Sun and Lilies, indicating that the individual has achieved a level of stability and fulfillment that allows them to bloom naturally. The symbol is deeply rooted in the concept of the balanced pair, where opposites are harmoniously present rather than in conflict, representing a state of peaceful acceptance where the past, present, and future are balanced. This state of equilibrium allows the individual to receive a "beautiful gift" or a "lucky break," suggesting that the journey has culminated in a moment of significant success or renewal. The bouquet is often depicted as a cluster of flowers arranged in a circle or a bunch, symbolizing unity and the interconnectedness of all parts, which reflects the belief that the individual has received a gift from the universe or a significant opportunity to begin a new chapter.

This symbol finds its deepest significance in the realms of love and relationships, where it signifies a successful relationship or a new beginning that has been nurtured. It suggests deep emotional connection, mutual respect, and a harmonious partnership, often indicating that the individual has received a gift of love or a new opportunity arising in a relationship. In the context of work and business, the Bouquet represents success, recognition, and a "lucky break," suggesting that the individual may have achieved a major milestone, secured a promotion, or found a new business venture that brings financial stability and visibility. The symbolism extends to health and well-being, where it is linked to the Sun and Lilies, indicating that the body is in a state of good health, energy is flowing freely, and there is a natural, peaceful approach to wellness. Furthermore, in the domain of finances, the Bouquet can indicate financial stability and the ability to manage resources effectively, suggesting that the individual has a clear path to success and is likely to receive a "plentiful flow" of resources or a significant windfall.

The deeper meaning and themes of the Bouquet apply to various aspects of life, from the emotional depths of personal relationships to the tangible realities of professional and financial success. It is frequently associated with the concept of the rebirth, where a flower blooms after a long wait, representing the rebirth of life and the rebirth of the self. The symbol also embodies the idea of a balanced relationship where the "other" or the opposite is present and harmonious, rather than a conflict. This balance is crucial for the individual to feel secure and fulfilled, allowing them to live a life that is both stable and dynamic. The Bouquet is often interpreted as a gift given to oneself or others, implying that the individual has received a "lucky break" or a significant opportunity to start a new chapter in their life.
