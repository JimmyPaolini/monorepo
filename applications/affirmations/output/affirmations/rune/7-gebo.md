# Gebo (Partnership) Affirmations


## ✅ Finite Indicative Active Past Simple First Singular

1. I accepted the gifts of all my partnerships
2. I gave freely in my balanced exchanges
3. I recognized the flow of mutual giving and receiving

## ⭐ Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I receive gifts from all relationships freely
2. I give and take in balanced partnership now
3. I accept the flow of mutual exchange always

## ⏭️ Finite Indicative Active Future Simple First Singular

1. I will give freely in this partnership
2. I will receive with open hands always
3. I will accept all mutual exchanges gladly

## 🔄 Finite Indicative Active Present Perfect Progressive First Singular

1. I have been giving and receiving freely in this bond
2. I have been accepting all exchanges with open hands
3. I have been flowing with the cycle of mutual gifts

## 🎯 Finite Indicative Active Future Perfect Perfective First Singular

1. I will have given freely and received deeply in partnership
2. I will have balanced the scales through mutual exchange
3. I will have become openhearted in all reciprocal giving

## 🤝 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Plural

1. We accept all gifts in this exchange
2. We freely give and receive in partnership
3. We flow through mutual giving and receiving

## 👤 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple Third

1. They receive gifts through open hands and hearts
2. She balances giving and receiving freely now
3. The partnership flows with mutual generous exchange

## 💪 Finite Potential Active Present Simple First Singular

1. I can freely give and receive in this bond
2. I am able to accept all mutual exchanges now
3. I can flow with others in balanced partnership

## 🙏 Finite Optative Active First Singular

1. May I receive all gifts in this partnership
2. Would that I might freely give and accept now
3. If only I could flow with mutual exchange

## ❗ Finite Imperative Active Second Singular

1. Receive all gifts with open hands yourself
2. Give freely and accept all mutual exchange
3. Balance your giving and receiving always

## ❓ Finite Interrogative Active Present First Singular Negative

1. Am I not open to receive all gifts
2. Have I not freely given and accepted now
3. Can I not flow in this mutual exchange

## ♾️ Infinitive Active Simple

1. To receive freely with open hands
2. To give generously in true exchange
3. To flow with the rhythm of partnership

## 💡 Gerund Active

1. Giving openly creates deep mutual connection
2. Receiving freely builds balanced partnership
3. Sharing generously fosters true reciprocal flow

## 🌊 Finite Indicative Passive Present Simple First Singular

1. I am held by mutual giving and receiving
2. I am balanced by the flow of all exchanges
3. I am grounded by reciprocal partnership and grace

## 🔁 Finite Indicative Active Present Habitual First Singular

1. I always give and receive freely in partnership
2. I regularly accept life's gifts with open hands
3. I consistently balance giving and receiving now

## 🫷 Finite Indicative Active Present Simple First Singular Negative

1. I do not hold back my gifts from others
2. I am not separate from the flow of exchange
3. I do not fear receiving what I deserve
